Maxfiylik Siyosati (IjaraBor)

Oxirgi yangilangan sana: 24-may, 2026-yil

"IjaraBor" jamoasi sizning shaxsiy ma'lumotlaringiz xavfsizligini ta'minlashni o'zining ustuvor vazifasi deb biladi. Ushbu siyosat Play Marketning eng so'nggi talablariga muvofiq ishlab chiqilgan.

1. Biz yig'adigan ma'lumotlar

  • Shaxsiy ma'lumotlar: Ism, telefon raqami va profil rasmi (Firebase Auth orqali ro'yxatdan o'tish uchun).
  • Aniq Joylashuv (GPS): Ilovamizning asosiy funksiyasi "Hyper-local" xizmat bo'lgani uchun, sizga eng yaqin e'lonlarni ko'rsatish va masofani hisoblash uchun joylashuv ma'lumotlaridan foydalanamiz.
  • Kamera va Galereya: E'lon joylash jarayonida mahsulot rasmlarini yuklash uchun ushbu ruxsatnomalar talab qilinadi.
  • Chat xabarlari: Foydalanuvchilar o'rtasidagi muloqotni ta'minlash uchun chat xabarlari Firestore bazasida xavfsiz saqlanadi.

2. Ma'lumotlardan foydalanish maqsadi

Yig'ilgan ma'lumotlar faqat quyidagilar uchun ishlatiladi:

  • Ilova funksiyalarini ta'minlash (E'lon berish, qidirish, xarita).
  • Xavfsizlikni ta'minlash va firibgarlikni oldini olish.
  • Meta (Facebook) Ads orqali ilovani optimallashtirish.
  • Firebase Crashlytics orqali texnik xatolarni tuzatish.

3. Ma'lumotlarni uchinchi tomonlarga berish

Biz ma'lumotlarni sotmaymiz. Faqatgina xizmat ko'rsatish uchun quyidagi texnologiyalardan foydalanamiz:

  • Google Firebase (Baza va Avtorizatsiya)
  • Cloudinary (Rasmlarni saqlash)
  • Meta SDK (Analitika va Reklama)

4. Ma'lumotlarni o'chirish (Data Deletion)

Foydalanuvchilar o'z ma'lumotlarini to'liq nazorat qilish huquqiga ega. Siz istalgan vaqtda:

  • Ilova ichidagi "Profilni o'chirish" tugmasi orqali hisobingizni o'chirishingiz mumkin.
  • Barcha shaxsiy ma'lumotlar, e'lonlar va rasmlar 30 kun ichida bazadan butunlay yo'q qilinadi.

Privacy Policy (IjaraBor)

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IjaraBor ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and profile picture provided via Firebase Auth.
  • Precise Location (GPS): We collect precise location data to provide nearby rental listings and calculate distances between users.
  • Camera and Gallery: Required for uploading photos when creating rental listings.
  • Communications: In-app chat messages are securely stored to facilitate communication between renters and owners.

2. Data Safety & Usage

We use the collected data to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including security monitoring and advertising optimization via Meta SDK.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Users have the right to delete their accounts at any time. Upon request via the "Delete Account" button in settings, all user data, including listings and photos, will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

We share data only with trusted service providers: Google Firebase, Cloudinary, and Meta (Facebook) Ads.
